This is sample BigARTM dataset.
It's purpose it to demonstrate how to provide datasets for VisARTM.
It consists only from two documents, but provides: 
 - raw document text;
 - metadata;
 - wordpos file;
 - Vowpal Wabbit file;
 - vocab.txt file.
 
For more information onn formats, refer to VisARTM documentation.
Don't use this dataset to create models.
